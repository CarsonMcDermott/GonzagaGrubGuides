Top 5 Restaurants

Frugals

Rating: 4.6

Frugals is a small diner that sells cheap cheeseburgers, chicken snadwhiches, fries, and shakes. Located on the east side of campus on campus side of hamilton straight down sharp.

Indeginous Eats

Rating: 4.6

Indeginous eats is a Native Amerian owned restaurant with amazing food. They are located accross the street from the zag shop on the north side.

Zekes Pizza

Rating: 4.2

Delicious artisan pizza with a great atmosphere. They have a great selction of pizza and the service is great. Located a block north of Dominos on the east side of hamilton.
